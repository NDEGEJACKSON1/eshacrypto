MARKET STRUCTURE

The most important thing you need as a good trader it's the ability to read the market structure,
which it's the skill that will allow you to use the price action in the market condition.
You can not trade all the market in the same way, the important skill you need is to understand the behavior of the market, which show how the market moves.

Three markets you need to experience;
1.Trending markets
2.Ranging markets and
3.Choppy markets

Trending market:

The trending markets it is characterized by repeating pattern

higher high and Higher low which is an up trending market, and Lower high and lower low which it's said to downtrend market. No need to complicate whether the market it's bullish or bearish only you can observe the price action is enough to show the trending of the market.

UP TRENDING
If you can understand how to identify the trending of the market it will be easy for you to trade, if it's the bullish trending then you will look for buying opportunity because you should trade the trend, you need to know when the corrective moves ends, and when the impulsive move begins,this we should use support and resistance,

DOWN TRENDING
It's characterized by repeating patterns lower high and lower low Let see an example of up trending market, Trending market it's easy you don't need to complicate you analysis, use your brain to understand what the market doing. In order to identify the trending of the market you suppose to use bigger timeframe like 4hrs, daily chart, weekly chart, these also will help to get strong support and resistance,but don't use smaller timeframes. Mostly of the opportunity in the down trend is the selling.. Professional traders understand how trending markets move; they always buy at the beginning of an impulsive move and take profits at the end of it.

Ranging market

If you look at any forex pair, or any time frame, you will

see that when price moves strongly in one direction, it makes strong impulsive moves, followed by smaller corrective moves that are pointing against the direction of where the pair is clearly going. This type of movement generates higher highs and higher lows in case of an uptrend and lower highs and lower lows in a case of a downtrend. A ranging market is one that it is not behaving like this. A sideway market is characterized by the fact that price is not making new highs and lows any more. instead it begins to make swing lows horizontally.see the chart below;

Choppy market

Choppy or consolidation markets are those which have no

clear directions, when you open your chart, and you find a lot of noise, you can’t even decide if the market is ranging, or trending. You have to know that you are watching a choppy market. This type of markets can make you feel very emotional and doubt your trading strategies as it starts to drop in performance. The best way to determine if a market is choppy is just by zooming out on the daily chart and taking in the bigger picture
